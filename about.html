Project inspiration

Going to the hospital and being delayed or not getting to meet with the doctor has
led to loss of lives of many. As a result we decided to build a site where one can
book an appointment with a doctor There's also means of identification if the
meeting is based on emergency or not. With this there's high reduction in
delay and treatment denial. because the patient's medical records
are all contained or collated during the appointment booking.
